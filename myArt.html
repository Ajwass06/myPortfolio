Bee Mural 2019-2021


I designed, sketched, and painted a 15x15ft mural. The goal of the project was to represent the core values of the Art, Science, and Design building at my highschool. I chose to depict a bee hive as it captures the perfect overlap of these core values. Bees use science and design to build their complex hives, creating beautiful, unique structures that can be considered true works of art. A bee hive is an active hub where science and art are used interchangeably to support a thriving ecosystem.
